# Funding And Payment

## Scope

Use this overlay for:

- top up
- payment setup
- account funding confirmation

## Emphasis

- step clarity
- selection visibility
- error prevention

## Preferred Modules

- `Payment Stepper`
- `Amount Input`
- `Amount Presets`
- `Payment Method List`
- `Top Up Destination`
- `Primary Button`

## Copy Tone

- explicit
- confidence-building
- low ambiguity

## Avoid

- mixed CTA hierarchy
- hidden selected states
- decorative modules that interrupt the flow
